GradTech is a professional certificate programme offered by IAPL Institute (International Academy of Professional Learning) for working professionals in Engineering disciplines. IAPL Institute is an accredited institutional member of ICDE (International Council for Open and Distance Education, icde.org) — a UNESCO Category II membership network for distance-education providers. This is a certificate programme, not a university degree; IAPL Institute is not a college or university and does not award degrees. Outcomes shown are individual graduate accounts and are not guaranteed. Fees and intake dates are indicative — confirm current details with your regional Student Advisor before enrolling.
